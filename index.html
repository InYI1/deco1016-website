Is Career coaching worth the monkey?

A career coach can’t guarantee to coach you to your career goal. They can be highly skilled and give it their all but they can’t make it absolute.Therefore we have a list of how Career Hunter is worth your money and time :

If you are currently unemployed

We can help you better prepare and showcase your work. we can work with you to develop a job search strategy, aim at the hidden job market and improve your confidence/expression. The sooner you work, the sooner you have income. If your career coaching goal is to get you to work faster, then the coach's return may be like this. This will only cost your first few days of salary.

If you are having your own business

A professional coach can help you get rid of the routine and see where you are going. They provide you with new paths to explore and help you try out what can be tried and what can't be tried in the business world. What is the standard? What are the exceptions? What are your requirements and expectations for your work? What can't you do? They can help you design your current job. They can help you through the middle age career crisis.
